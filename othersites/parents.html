Advice for Parents

How to Prevent Your Child from Getting Cyberbullied

  • Parental Controls: use parental controls to prevent your child from being exposed to the bad side of the internet
  • Monitoring: make sure you are aware of what websites your child visits and uses

Teaching Proper Online Behavior

As parents, not only is it important to keep your children away from cyberbullies, but it is important to teach your child proper online etiquette to prevent them from becoming cyberbullies. Below are some tips to teach your child to practice good behavior online.

  • Language: make sure that your child doesn’t use bad words
  • Personal Information: make sure that your child knows what information is ok to share online and what isn’t
  • Raising Upstanders: teach your child to stand up for themselves and others
  • Report: ensure your child is comfortable to reporting cyberbullying to you
  • Empathy: your child should only post online what they would say in person
