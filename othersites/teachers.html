Advice for Teachers

Is One of Your Students Getting Cyberbullied?

Below are some warning signs that one of your students is possibly getting cyberbullied.

  • Obvious changes (increased or decreased) in the amount of time spent on their device
  • The student is hiding their device when they people are around
  • Student seems depressed
  • Grades are dropping and less motivated to do work
  • No longer is social and spends a lot of time alone

What Can You do?

  • Spread awareness in the classroom and teach students proper online etiquette
  • Talk to the student and offer support
  • Encourage upstanding
  • Talk the student's parents
  • Create and maintain a no-bully environment at school that can transfer onto the online environment
